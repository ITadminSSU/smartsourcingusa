# Staff Payroll Portal — Employee Guide

Welcome! This short guide explains how to log in and use the Smart Sourcing USA
staff payroll portal to submit your hours and get your invoices.

> Tip: the text below can be copied into a welcome email for new staff. Replace
> `https://smartsourcingusa.com` with your live domain if different.

---

## 1. Your login details
Your admin will give you:
- **Portal link:** https://smartsourcingusa.com/portal/login
- **Username**
- **Temporary password**

## 2. First login
1. Open the portal link and sign in with your username + temporary password.
2. You'll be asked to **set your own password**. It must be:
   - at least 8 characters, with an uppercase letter, a lowercase letter, and a number.
3. You'll then be asked to add your **bank details** (encrypted — only you and
   accounting can see them). These are used to pay your invoices.

## 3. Submitting a timesheet
1. From the dashboard, open **Timesheets → New timesheet**.
2. Choose your **pay period** (coverage start and end dates).
3. Add your work entries (date, hours, and trade/client if applicable).
4. Click **Submit** when done. Your approver is notified automatically.

## 4. After approval
- Once approved, an **invoice is generated automatically** from your hours.
- Open **Invoices** to view it and **Download PDF** for your records.

## 5. Your profile
Use **Profile** (top right) any time to:
- Update your name or email
- Change your password
- Update your bank details

## 6. Forgot your password?
On the login page, click **Forgot password?**, enter your username or email, and
you'll receive a reset link by email (valid for 1 hour).

## Notes on security
- After several wrong password attempts, your account locks for 15 minutes — just
  wait and try again, or use **Forgot password?**.
- Never share your password. Staff only ever see their own information; accounting
  and admins manage payroll.

Questions? Contact your team lead or the accounting team.
